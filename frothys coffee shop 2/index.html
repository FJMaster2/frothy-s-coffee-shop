About Us


Richard, Louise & their team at Frothys Coffee Shop has been proudly serving Stamford since 2007. Since then, our mission has been to provide freshly prepared food to order on the premises using quality, locally sourced produce whenever possible. Vegetarians, vegans & gluten free dietary needs are all catered for. Decaffeinated & dairy milk alternatives are available. We pride ourselves on our welcoming, friendly customer service. Frothys is licensed, so you can enjoy Draught & Bottled Beer, a glass of wine, Cider or Baileys latte. There is plenty of space, a great vintage map on the wall & work from local artists taking pride of place. There's seating outside & a roaring fire to suit all weathers. Newspapers & magazines are on display for your perusal.


Try it yourself, stop by today!



Menu


The chef-driven menu highlights ingredients sourced locally whenever possible. Our suppliers, to name but a few, include Askers Bakery, Nelsons Butchers, Colin Fisheries, Peterborough Dairy and Premier Produce for fresh seasonal vegetables to create simple, handcrafted dishes served straight from our kitchen.
